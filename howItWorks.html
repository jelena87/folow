Safe search & restrictions

Feel safe on our platform

A majour thing we care about at folow, is not just your privacy and safety, but also that of our younger audiances and those that just don't want to see things in the social space such as adult related items, profanity and so on. This is why we introduced safe search as standard, this feature can be disabled however to do so account must be verified and be over the age of 18 with a valid Driving Licence, Passport or Debit/Credit card. We have put this in place not just for safety and peace of mind but to also avoid fake profiles and spammy profiles from creating fake news content or being able to set up subscriptions. This gives all our users protection and peace of mind.
